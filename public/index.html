About Me

Hey! I'm Anne-Lynn, the person behind this blog. It’s my desire to share the goodness of God the Father with the world. Over the last few years, He has completely changed my life and taken me on a personal journey to discover how we can truly live from our identity in Christ and walk daily in the newness of life He’s given us.



Having grown up in the Netherlands for twenty years, I moved to Germany last year to attend the Awakening School of Ministry. This summer, the Lord put it on my heart to share with you—through this blog—what He is doing in and through my life.
Find out more about me and my story on the “About” page.
