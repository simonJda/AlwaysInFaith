Privacy Policy

The protection of your personal data is important to us. Below we inform you about which personal data we collect and how we use it.

1. Responsible party

Simon Jarda
Creator of this website / Responsible for data processing
Email: s.jarda@web.de

2. Data we collect

When you use the login function on this website, we collect the following data:

  • IP address
  • Number of login attempts
  • Timestamps of login attempts

3. Purpose and legal basis

The collected data is used exclusively for security purposes, for example to:

  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Limit repeated incorrect login attempts (brute-force protection)

The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest), as the processing is necessary to protect the website and its users from misuse.

4. Storage period

Login-related data (IP, attempt count, timestamps) is stored only temporarily and is automatically deleted or reset after a short retention period (e.g., 2 hours).

5. Disclosure to third parties

Your data will not be shared with third parties. Data is processed only on servers used by this website and is not sold or otherwise disclosed.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (where legally permitted)
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
